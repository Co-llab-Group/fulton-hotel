Section 01

Executive Summary

The Opportunity

Convert an existing 3-story hotel at 2208-2210 Fulton Avenue into 16 premium micro-suites (12 standard + 4 loft) targeting traveling professionals, healthcare workers, and extended-stay guests. The property is already owned (zero acquisition cost), and zoning (RMX-MH) allows by-right conversion with no parking minimums and no density limits.

The Numbers

  • Total Project Cost: $1.72M
  • Capital Stack: 75% debt / 25% equity ($429K)
  • Year 1 NOI: ~$269K
  • Stabilized NOI: ~$384K
  • Stabilized Cash-on-Cash: 43.5%
  • DSCR: 1.54x (Yr 1) → 2.50x (Stabilized)
  • Construction: 9 months
  • Breakeven: ~65% occupancy

The Recommendation

Proceed to design development. The deal fundamentals are strong—zero basis, favorable zoning, multiple revenue channels (nightly, weekly, monthly), and a stabilized cash-on-cash well above market.

Key risks (occupancy ramp, construction costs) are manageable with the 10% contingency and conservative underwriting.

Section 03

The Concept

Premium micro-suites designed for modern professionals who need more than a hotel room but less than a full apartment. Each unit is a self-contained living space with kitchenette, workspace, and private bathroom.

Unit Mix

TypeCountSizeNightly RateFeatures
Standard Suite12~300 SF$139Kitchenette, workspace, full bath
Loft Suite4~500 SF$189Two-level, living area + sleeping loft

Revenue Model

  • Nightly: Airbnb, Booking.com, direct ($139-189/night)
  • Weekly: 15% discount ($118-161/night)
  • Monthly: 35% discount ($90-123/night)
  • Target mix: 25% nightly / 25% weekly / 50% monthly
  • Channels: Airbnb, Furnished Finder, direct booking, corporate contracts

Target Market

  • Traveling nurses & healthcare professionals (nearby hospitals)
  • Business travelers & consultants
  • Relocating professionals (month-to-month flexibility)
  • Visiting faculty & researchers
